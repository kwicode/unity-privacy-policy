Privacy Policy for Unitly

Effective Date: 14 March 2026

Unitly ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share when using our mobile application and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or use Unitly, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account login details
  • Property ownership or tenancy information

Property and Tenant Information

Unitly is a property management platform that allows users to manage properties and tenants. Users may store information such as:

  • Property details
  • Tenant information
  • Lease agreements
  • Payment records
  • Maintenance requests

Device Information

We may collect certain technical information automatically, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostics data

2. App Permissions

Unitly may request certain permissions from your device.

Camera

Used to capture documents, receipts, or property images when submitting maintenance requests or records.

Storage / Media Access

Used to upload documents or images related to property management.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide property management services
  • Enable communication between landlords and tenants
  • Track rental payments and maintenance requests
  • Improve app functionality and reliability
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers who help operate the platform
  • When required by law or legal processes
  • With authorized users within the same property management structure

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information including secure servers, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access controls.

However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Children's Privacy

Unitly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
