Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Unitly account and associated data, you may request account deletion using the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email from your registered email address to:

support@unitly.app

Include the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • A request stating that you would like your Unitly account deleted

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request, we will process your account deletion within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted.

  • Your user account
  • Personal information
  • Associated property and tenant records

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained if required for legal or financial record-keeping purposes.

For more information please review our Privacy Policy.
